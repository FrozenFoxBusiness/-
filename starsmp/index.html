Falling Star Ritual

How the Ritual Works

1. Starting the Ritual
- Drop a special star item near the Falling Star Lodestone (within 3 blocks)
- The star will begin orbiting the pillar
- A 20-minute countdown starts

2. During the Ritual
- A boss bar appears for all players showing time remaining
- The server broadcasts the ritual coords every 5 minutes

3. Completion
- After 20 minutes, the star transforms into a "Fused" version
- The star drops at the location of the Lodestone
- Each type of star can only be fused once

Trust System

How Trust Works

- Use /trust [player] to add players to your trust list
- Use /untrust [player] to remove them
- Trusted players cannot be harmed by your star abilities

Key Features

- Prevents accidental team damage
- Works across all star types
- Trust list persists between logins
- Admin commands available for testing

Technical Details

- Trust data is saved in trust_data.yml
- Player names are cached for offline viewing
- Automatic data saving every 15 minutes
