Star SMP Plugin

A SMP with 12 star types, each giving distinct abilities

LIFE-STAR

Overworld - Haste 1

Passive

4 extra hearts

Ability: Life Drain

Gain half a heart per crit for 20 seconds (60s cooldown)

Ultimate: Total Restoration

Full heal and saturation for you and trusted allies (120s cooldown)

ICE-STAR

Overworld - Haste 1

Passive

Speed 5 on ice + every 8 hits 50% chance to apply slowness 4

Ability: Ice Ring

Creates an ice domain for 15 seconds made from blue ice (60s cooldown)

Ultimate: Sub Zero

Full stuns and holds enemies in place for 10s (105s cooldown)

First-person view

Replay view

THUNDER-STAR

Overworld - Haste 1

Passive

Speed 2 every 15 hits thunder strike

Ability: Bolt

Shoot a thunderbolt that disables stars for 30s and does 4 hearts (80s cooldown)

Ultimate: Always Strike Lightning

Every hit = thunder strike for 8 seconds and speed 4 (105s cooldown)

SOUND-STAR

Overworld - Haste 1

Passive

Ultimate has 2 forms (sneaking/standing)

Ability: Sword Sing

Movements with sword as fluid as with a whip for 10 seconds. Each successful hit increases cooldown of hit enemy by 10s

Ultimate

Form 1 (Standing): AOE around you that flings people away (4 hearts damage)
Form 2 (Sneaking): Forward attack that increases cooldown of hit players by 15s (5.5 hearts damage)

Replay view

FIRE-STAR

Nether - Fire Res

Passive

Every 12 hits 50% chance to set enemy on fire + /togglefireglow

Ability: Fireflies

Shoot out 6 fireflies that home on closest enemy for first 2 seconds, setting them on fire and dealing 1 heart on hit (80s cooldown)

Ultimate: Fireball

Shoot a fireball that deals 7 hearts of damage (90s cooldown)

STRENGTH-STAR

Nether - Fire Res

Passive

Strength 1

Ability: Rage

Lose hearts for 30s of strength 2 (75s cooldown)

Ultimate: Revenge Counter

Charge ultimate's damage by taking damage from players for first 10s, then deal all accumulated damage in one hit (90s cooldown)

WITHER-STAR

Nether - Fire Res

Passive

Every 8 hits 50% chance to apply wither 2 for 3s and gain 2 extra hearts for 10s

Ability: Decay

Takes 3 perm hearts from every player in 5 block radius for 40s and inflicts wither in AOE for 20s (75s cooldown)

Ultimate: Wither Head

Shoots 3 wither skeleton heads each dealing 1 heart damage and applying wither (90s cooldown)

GHOST-STAR

Nether - Fire Res

Passive

Every 10 hits 20% chance to take 0 damage

Ability: Haunt

Most recently hit player gets slowness and blindness, stars pulse glowing for 15s (90s cooldown)

Ultimate: Ghost Form

Turn into ghost for 20s - immunity to melee (not bows/fireballs/dash) and true invisibility (105s cooldown)

MATRIX-STAR

End - Chorus Fruit Gaps

Passive

On half health gain resistance 1

Ability: Disable

Disable wind charges for 8 seconds (60s cooldown)

Ultimate: Screen Glitch

For 10s all enemies in 3 block AOE get blinded (via texture pack) and user gains haste 10 (105s cooldown)

VOID-STAR

End - Chorus Fruit Gaps

Passive

Pushes you out of void + /togglevoidinvis

Ability: Chain

Chain enemies to ground (poison effect) for 15s (75s cooldown)

Ultimate: Binding Void

Chains enemies to you and blinds them (15s), blinds their hearts for 25s (90s cooldown)

AIR-STAR

End - Chorus Fruit Gaps

Passive

No fall damage

Ability: Tornado

Suck every player in 15 block radius and spit them out (60s cooldown)

Ultimate: Dash

Dash forward dealing 2.5 hearts, hiding hearts briefly and applying poison (45s cooldown)

PURPLE-STAR

End - Chorus Fruit Gaps

Passive

Every 15 hits 50% chance to deal 1.5x damage

Ability: Marked for Death

Marks 1 enemy in 20 block range for death (shadow step multiplier increases to 2.5x) (80s cooldown)

Ultimate: Shadow Step

Teleport behind enemy in 20 block radius, next hit deals 2x damage (105s cooldown)
